Experiments

CodaLab experiments feature an interactive computation environment where code, text, mathematics, and other rich media are combined into a single document. CodaLab experiments enable researchers to quickly sketch ideas and collaborate.

Competitions

CodaLab competitions provide an opportunity for researchers, developers and algorithmists to create solutions for problems across a wide range of domains, and advance the state of the art for their respective areas of interest.

CodaLab

CodaLab is a platform built using Python, Django, and Foundation that lets communities explore experiments together and propose challenges designed to advance the state of the art. The first two communities helping build CodaLab are the Machine Learning community and the Medical Imaging community.
